# 🌙 Sleep Health & Lifestyle: A Predictive Analysis 📊

## 📖 Project Overview
What actually determines a "good night's sleep"? Is it just duration, or do our daily stress levels and occupational choices play a larger role? 

This project performs a deep-dive analysis into the **Sleep Health and Lifestyle Dataset**, a comprehensive collection of 374 entries covering 13 lifestyle and health variables. I developed a multi-layered Power BI dashboard to uncover how daily habits correlate with clinical sleep disorders such as **Insomnia** and **Sleep Apnea**.

## 🧠 Strategic Insights & Findings
Through data transformation and visualization, I identified several critical correlations:
* **The Stress-Sleep Paradox:** A clear inverse relationship exists where high stress levels (8+) drastically reduce sleep quality, regardless of total sleep duration.
* **Occupational Risk Profiles:** Analysis revealed that specific professions (e.g., Nurses and Salespeople) show a significantly higher prevalence of Sleep Apnea compared to Engineers and Accountants.
* **The Physical Activity 'Sweet Spot':** Data suggests that moderate-to-high physical activity (60+ mins/day) correlates with an 18% improvement in perceived sleep quality.

## 🛠️ The Tech Stack
* **Analytics & Visualization:** Power BI (DAX, Power Query)
* **Data Processing:** Cleaned and structured raw CSV data to handle categorical variables (BMI Category, Occupation).
* **Documentation:** Synthesized findings into a structured technical report (Article Writing).
